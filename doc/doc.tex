\section{Wavelength definition}

\texttt{Wavelength}: Measurement of one cycle of the frequency.

\subsection{Formula}

\begin{displaymath}
\lambda = \frac{c}{f\sqrt{\epsilon}}
\end{displaymath}

$\lambda$: wavelength of a signal

$c$: speed of light

$f$: frequency

$\epsilon$: relative dielectric constant
